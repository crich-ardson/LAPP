About

Hi there, my name's Cameron. If you're not my girlfriend, feel free to click around. However, know that this content is in no way, shape, or form meant for you.


I originally planned for this project to be a music player, as it would hold all the songs I've sung in one easy place. Instead, it's become a little experiment with some HTML, CSS, and Javascript to make a soundboard that puts out my voice at the click of a button. When you've got time and I'm not immediately nearby, my voice is right here to keep you company.
